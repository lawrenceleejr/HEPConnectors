Field cheats

M vs FMale carries the pin/centre-conductor; female has the socket. Barrels & gender-changers bridge like-to-like.
50 vs 75 ΩBNC comes in both — 50 Ω for data/RF & NIM logic, 75 Ω for video/RF-over-coax. Don't mix on long runs.
APC ≠ UPCNever mate a green (angled) ferrule to a blue (flat) one — you'll wreck the end-face and the return loss.
SM ≠ MMYellow single-mode and aqua/orange multimode don't interoperate. Match the glass, not just the connector.
NIM / TTLfast logic & triggers ride LEMO & BNC
DAQfibre + QSFP carry the read-out uplinks
